University College TATI 

Place Study Now 

It started out in a temporary campus in Sultan Zainal Abidin University (formely known as KUSZA), Kuala Terengganu in July 1994.

UC TATI was relocated to the permanent campus in Teluk Kalong, Kemaman in July 1996. The establishment of UC TATI was not only an initiative of state government but was also as a result of cooperation from federal government and private companies.

It is a continuous effort to fill the best quality manpower in the market place. This collaboration established a multi-focal management team that is not only responsible in managing but also financing TATIUC establishment

Pusat Pendidikan Yayasan Terengganu Besut

Place Study For SPM 

PPYT was established in the 1980s to help Terengganu State students who dropped out in the public examination for the purpose of qualifying them to continue their education higher level in selected study centers.

PPYT is an upper secondary education institution that offers a place to study for students who sit for the Malaysian Certificate of Education exam (SPM) under the management of the Terengganu State Government through the Terengganu Foundation.
